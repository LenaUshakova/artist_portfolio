About me

Hi, my name is Olena and I am an artist from Ukraine based in Toronto, Canada.

I have always had a passion for art, especially to the techniques of oil and watercolor. In fact, I'm pretty sure I was born with a paintbrush in my hand.

My work is inspired by travels and nature, and I'm the kind of person who gets excited about trying out new techniques and materials, even if it means ruining a few canvases along the way. Hey, all part of the artistic process, isn't it?

I hope that my art brings joy and inspiration to those who view it.

If you have any questions or are interested in purchasing one of my paintings, please don't hesitate to reach out.

Thank you for visiting my page. Hopefully you will like what you see!
